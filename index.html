Improving battery energy density & cost

Ouros introduces a novel cathode material that boasts the highest capacity ever recorded, revolutionizing the landscape of energy storage. By utilizing a denser cathode material, it enables the thickening of each anode layer, resulting in enhanced capacity per layer while simultaneously reducing inactive mass. It offers significant cost advantages compared to existing alternatives. Additionally, Ouros streamlines cathode manufacturing processes by eliminating 80% of the associated steps, promising increased efficiency and scalability in production.
